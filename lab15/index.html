Lab 15: Fancy CSS 1

Big Ideas

The subject of this lab is to get more familiar with CSS using more fancier CSS methods. In this lab we added more sophistication to our webpages by adding text-shadows, box-shadows, gradients, filters, rounded corners, object-fit, and transform. These techniques made our webpage look fancier than before.

Challenges

Overall, this lab was very successful and we didn't have many problems. The only challenge we had was adding a graident. However, we were able to quickly resolve this issue by realizing we made tiny formatting mistakes.
